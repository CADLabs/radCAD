# Tutorials

Tutorials are **learning-oriented**: they take you by the hand through a series of steps to build something, so you learn how radCAD works by doing.

Start here if you are new to radCAD:

1. **[Installation](installation.md)**: get radCAD installed and verify it works.
2. **[Build your first model](first-model.md)**: model a simple growth system from scratch, run it, and inspect the results.

Once you are comfortable, move on to the [How-to guides](../how-to/index.md) for task-focused guides, or the [Explanation](../explanation/index.md) section to understand the concepts behind what you built.

!!! tip "Prefer a full course?"
    [cadCAD.education](https://cadcad.education) offers a beginner course for the cadCAD/radCAD modelling approach.
